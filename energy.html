Our Energy & Power Services

We deliver comprehensive power security, sustainable generation, and efficient management systems to secure your operations and accelerate your green transition.

I. Critical Power Security (UPS & ATS)

Providing uninterrupted operations for mission-critical loads, data centers, and industrial environments using high-reliability UPS systems and intelligent power transfer solutions.

  • Rack-Based UPS Solutions: Seamless backup power and power management.
  • 3-Phase Modular UPS: High-capacity, scalable, and stable power for critical loads.
  • Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS): Ensures power continuity by automatically switching between primary and backup sources.
  • Power Distribution Units (PDUs): Cost-effective distribution, monitoring, and management of power within data racks.

II. Sustainable Energy Generation

Invest in tailored renewable solutions that provide independence and a guaranteed supply of clean energy for commercial and residential applications.

  • Solar Panel Installation: Turnkey services including design, installation, and maintenance of photovoltaic modules.
  • Hybrid Inverters: Combines grid-tied efficiency with off-grid security, allowing you to feed back excess power.
  • Off-Grid Inverters: Enables complete energy independence for stand-alone systems.
  • Quality Solar Inverters: Tried and tested components meeting residential and commercial solar energy requirements.

III. Advanced Storage & Efficiency

Selecting the right battery technology is essential for reliable backup and renewable energy systems. We offer leading options for every application.

  • Lithium-Iron-Phosphate (LiFePO4) Batteries: The preferred storage for high efficiency, reliability, and long cycle life.
  • AGM Deep-Cycle Batteries: Maintenance-free, spill-proof batteries ideal for solar backup power systems due to low self-discharge.
  • Lead-Acid Batteries: A cost-effective and reliable option for applications requiring high power output and frequent cycling.
