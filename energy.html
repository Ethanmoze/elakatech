UPS Solutions

Our UPS solutions provide protection from power surges and interruptions.

  • Single-phase: Provides power protection for electronic equipment, guarding against voltage fluctuations and outages. Suitable for residential, commercial, and light industrial applications.
  • 3-phase Modular: Delivers high efficiency and reliability with a modular architecture. Ideal for larger applications like Data Centers.
  • Rack Based: Provides reliable backup power for data centers and ensures uninterrupted operations during power source transitions.

Solar Inverters

Tried and tested solar inverters designed for residential or commercial applications.

  • Hybrid Inverters: Combine grid-tied and off-grid functions. They can connect to the grid to feed back excess power or use stored power during outages.
  • Off-Grid Inverters: Operate as stand-alone systems, allowing you to generate and store your own electricity.

Battery Storage¬†

  • Lithium Batteries: The preferred storage technology for a reliable supply of renewable energy. Ideal for industrial, commercial, and residential use.
  • AGM Batteries: Advanced, maintenance-free, sealed, and spill-free lead-acid batteries. Ideal for solar backup due to low self-discharge and long lifespan.
  • Lead-acid Batteries: Durable, reliable, and deliver high power output. They are generally less expensive than other types.

Power Management

  • Power Distribution Units (PDUs): Allow for reliable distribution, protection, and management of power to multiple devices in Data Center environments.
  • Automatic Transfer Switches (ATS): Ensure continuous delivery of electrical power from one of two sources. When the main source fails, the ATS switches to the backup source to prevent downtime.
